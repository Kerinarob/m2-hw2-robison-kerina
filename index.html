My Favorite Hobby: Consuming Media

Introduction | Why I Love Media | Favorite Books | Concerts | Resources

Introduction

My favorite hobby is consuming media in all forms. I love playing video games, reading books, listening to music and creating playlists, watching reality television, and staying informed through the news. Media is something I spend a lot of my free time engaging with because there is always something new to discover.

Each type of media offers something different. Some forms are relaxing and entertaining, while others help me understand different perspectives and stay connected to the world around me.

Why I Love Media

I have always loved reading, and growing up with two older brothers meant video games became a big part of my life early on. Some of my favorite childhood games included The Sims and Minecraft, and I still enjoy discovering new games today. Last weekend I started playing the new Tomodachi Life for the Nintendo Switch and have been having a great time with it so far.

Music was also always present growing up because my dad has been a lifelong steering wheel drummer. Listening to music and creating playlists became one of my favorite ways to express emotions and connect with other people’s experiences through their words and perspectives.

I also enjoy watching reality television, especially Bravo shows. While some people see reality TV as shallow entertainment, I find the interpersonal relationships and conflict really interesting to watch and analyze. I also try to stay engaged with politics and current events because they affect everyday life and help me stay informed about what is happening in the world.

My Favorite Books

  1. The Hunger Games series — Suzanne Collins
  2. The Stranger — Albert Camus
  3. The Perks of Being a Wallflower — Stephen Chbosky

Concerts and Live Music

This photo was taken at Lollapalooza in Chicago in 2025. It was one of my favorite concert experiences and I had an amazing time. I love going to concerts because seeing artists perform live creates a completely different experience from listening at home. Supporting artists and sharing live music experiences with other people makes concerts one of my favorite parts of consuming media.

Media Curation

As a media enthusiast, I have to admit that I actually love the brainrot that is TikTok. While it can definitely be distracting, I enjoy how quickly it exposes me to new music, books, pop culture conversations, news, and trends. It has become one of my favorite places to discover and engage with different forms of media.
